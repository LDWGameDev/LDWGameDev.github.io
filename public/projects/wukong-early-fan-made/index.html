Wukong Early Fan Made

2021

Dive headfirst into intense, action-packed battles in Myth of the Jade Monkey, an RPG inspired by the high-energy combat of Black Myth: Wukong's first trailer. Play as a fierce warrior armed with elemental powers, mastering fast, fluid combat techniques to conquer formidable, mythic foes.

Unreal Engine Personal

First project after switching to Unreal Engine. I tried to mess around with the framework and see how everything works together. To be honest, I was impressed but also overwhelmed at first by all the tools and features in front of me.

What I worked on:

  • Combat system.
  • Environment creation tools.
  • Enemy AI.
