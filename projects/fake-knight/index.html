Fake Knight

Fake Knight is a 2D action-combat adventure set in the Underneath, a forgotten realm fallen to monstrous foes. Explore winding tunnels, gear up with weapon and armor sets, take on quests from the survivors, and fight through melee, ranged, flying, and boss enemies to reclaim what was lost.

Unity Personal

My first — and, as it turned out, only — Unity project, built as the final assignment for a second-year university course. I moved to Unreal Engine right after finishing it. Made purely for learning, so it contains some copyrighted art and audio and isn't intended for release.

What I worked on:

  • Inventory and equipment with set bonuses.
  • Four enemy archetypes — melee, ranged, flying, and boss — built on a shared state machine that also drives the player.
  • Save/load with three save slots.
  • Quest system as proof of concept — all quests granted at start, completable in any order. Didn't have time to expand into a full questline.

Full playthrough.

Honestly, the systems were over-scoped for a coursework deadline — the state machine alone ended up the heaviest piece of engineering. I came out of it having learned more about cutting scope than about combat feel.
