Experience bar — ongoing fade-out treatments

Six options for tapering the right edge of an ongoing-job bar so the bar visibly does not have an end-cap. All variants use the Variant 4 base (dark bar + orange left strip + orange “● LIVE” chip) and replace any green accents with orange. The neutral bar on the left of each stage is a reference “ended” role.

Fade A — Gradient fade (background only)

Bar background gradients from solid to fully transparent over the last 45%. Border is redrawn only across the opaque region. Chip stays at the right, just outside the fade zone. Closest to “bar literally dissolves into nothing”.
