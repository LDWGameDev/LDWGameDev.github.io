Experience detail card — ongoing job treatments

Six mockups for the "currently active" state of the detail panel, using the Indie Game Developer entry as the example. All variants swap the green company text + green tech chips for the orange palette (#ff7a18) used by the WIP pills on the projects timeline. The goal is to communicate ongoing instead of showing a hard end date.

Variant A — Inline “Ongoing” pill

Smallest delta from today. Replaces the end date in the meta line with an orange pill containing a pulsing dot + “Ongoing”. Card chrome unchanged.
